How Aura Highlights & Scores:

Highlighting Words: The engine automatically parses text on the home screen against your personal registry. Positive or neutral keywords show up bright and stylized in absolute bold colors with no covers to maintain a readable scrapbook sheet.

Sound Generation: Hovering or tapping colored phrases in the list triggers immediate plucky minor or major keys built via custom envelopes to play only for a tiny fraction of time so sounds stay crisp.

Scoring day: The Energy bar captures physical/mental battery (1-10 slider), and the Harmony % measures the vocabulary depth, descriptor density, and structural variety in your logs.
